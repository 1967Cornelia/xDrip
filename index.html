xDrip

Use your Dexcom G4 with Android, WIRELESSLY!

View on GitHub Download .zip Download .tar.gz
** Please note this is __NOT_ a product created by or backed by Dexcom, you can check them out here **_

xDrip Currently Supports 2 different modes:

Wirelessly speaking with a Dexcom Share receiver -- OR -- Building your own wireless bridge and skip the receiver altogether

Access your Glucose levels from anywhere!

  • xDrip is ready to upload your glucose data to any NightScout server for remote monitoring on just about any internet connected device!! Never heard of it? Check out the Nightscout Home Page
  • Android Wear Support through NightWatch
  • Homescreen and Lockscreen Widget!

Want to learn more?? Jump on over to the Wiki for instructions and downloads!!
